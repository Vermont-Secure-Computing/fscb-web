About FSCB

PLEASE READ ALL AND UNDERSTAND BEFORE USE

The Free State Central Bank (FSCB) is open source software and comes with no warranty or guarantee of any kind.

The purpose of FSCB is to help organize cryptocurrency trusts, which store funds using multisignatures enabling a higher security envronment for long term wealth management. FSCB was created by a team of software consutants working with the Vermont Secure Computing Consultancy.

In a normal banking model, an account holder entrusts bank employees with custody of their funds. In the FSCB model, the account holder entrusts "bankers" from his own social networks to take partial custody, to hold the private keys that control the funds in the account.

The account holder can also choose the redundancy level of the trust, via how many keys will be necessary to save and move the funds. Because of this redundancy, if one or more keys are lost, the funds will not necessarily be lost.

There is no futher counterparty risk associated with such an account, no company or individual other than those selected by the account holder has any control of the keys.

FSCB software will never store any private keys, remember to back up all keys and information associated with your funds! Any methods can be used by selected bankers to store private keys.

FSCB software creators have no control of your funds nor responsibility of any funds.

FSCB is serverless, there are no centralized records of any kind maintained regarding the use of this software.

Use at your own risk!
